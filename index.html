The Story Continues...

Conrad Van Cottonmouth returns in this gripping sequel that plunges our serpentine sleuth into the darkest corners of Reptilia City. When a series of mysterious disappearances rocks the Amphibian District, Conrad must navigate a treacherous web of corruption that reaches the highest levels of the city's elite.

With his trusted secretary Liz Lizard and informant Gecko "The Eyes" Jones by his side, Conrad will face his most dangerous case yet. But as the venom trail leads closer to home, our hero must confront his own troubled past and the family secrets he's tried so hard to bury.

Book 2 expands our noir universe with 120 pages of stunning artwork, introducing new characters and deepening the rich mythology that fans have come to love.

Meet the Creator

Michael Connors

Michael Connors is an award-winning comic book artist and writer with over 15 years of experience in the industry. After working with major publishers on titles like "Midnight Shadows" and "The Extraordinary," Michael launched his independent career with Conrad Van Cottonmouth in 2020.

"Book 1 was a labor of love that exceeded all my expectations. The response from fans has been incredible, and I'm thrilled to continue Conrad's story with even more depth, intrigue, and the noir atmosphere readers have come to love. Your support makes it possible for independent creators like me to bring these unique stories to life."
